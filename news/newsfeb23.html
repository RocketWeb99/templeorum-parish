Clothing Collection
Donate your unwanted clobber to help ‘A Few Good Women’ raise money for the Pil River Park and our three schools.
We can accept: Clean, Dry, Reusable Clothes, Paired shoes, Handbags & Belts, Curtains and Bedding.  We cannot accept: Pillows or Duvets
Place your clobber in a bin bag, tie securely and drop to Piltown N.S., Owning N.S. or Templeorum N.S. before Monday, 9th March.
