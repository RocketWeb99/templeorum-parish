Annual Mass to remember Victims of Crime
You are invited to attend a special Mass to remember victims of crime on Sunday, 23rd February at 12noon in Knock Basilica. This Mass is in association with the Federation for Victim Assistance and supports European Victim of Crime Day to remember all who suffer at the hands of criminals throughout the island of Ireland and Europe. For more details please contact Mary in our Victim Assistance head office on (086)3938871 or Freephone 1 800 277 477.

Clothing Collection
Donate your unwanted clobber to help ‘A Few Good Women’ raise money for the Pil River Park and our three schools.
We can accept: Clean, Dry, Reusable Clothes, Paired shoes, Handbags & Belts, Curtains and Bedding.  We cannot accept: Pillows or Duvets
Place your clobber in a bin bag, tie securely and drop to Piltown N.S., Owning N.S. or Templeorum N.S. before Monday, 9th March.
